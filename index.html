Our Mission & History

Founded in 1992 in Dhaka, Bangladesh by Dr. Humaira Islam, Shakti Foundation emerged from a vision to create lasting change for underprivileged women. What began as a pilot microfinance program in urban slums has grown into one of Bangladesh's largest NGOs.

Over the past three decades, we have expanded across 57 districts, serving over 475,199 members. Our comprehensive approach addresses the root causes of poverty through sustainable programs that empower women and strengthen communities.

We believe in sustainable development that strengthens communities from within, fostering self-reliance and long-term prosperity through:

  • Microfinance and economic empowerment
  • Healthcare access and medical services
  • Education and scholarship programs
  • Skills training and vocational development
  • Community advocacy and support
