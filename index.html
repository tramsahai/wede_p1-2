Our Mission & History

Founded in 1992 in Dhaka, Bangladesh by Dr. Humaira Islam, Shakti Foundation emerged from a vision to create lasting change for underprivileged women...

  • Microfinance and economic empowerment
  • Healthcare access and medical services
  • Education and scholarship programs
  • Skills training and vocational development
  • Community advocacy and support
