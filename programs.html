Microfinance Program Details

Our microfinance initiative provides small business loans ranging from ₹10,000 to ₹50,000 with flexible repayment terms. We offer financial literacy workshops covering budgeting, savings, and business management. The program specifically targets women entrepreneurs in rural areas, helping them establish sustainable income sources and achieve financial independence.

Healthcare Program Details

Our healthcare program operates mobile clinics that visit remote villages weekly, providing free medical checkups, vaccinations, and essential medications. We focus on maternal and child health, offering prenatal care, immunization drives, and nutrition counseling. Our community health workers conduct door-to-door visits to ensure follow-up care and health education.

Education Program Details

The education initiative provides scholarships for underprivileged children, school infrastructure development including computer labs and libraries, and teacher training programs. We also run adult literacy classes in evenings, helping parents gain basic reading and writing skills. Our after-school tutoring programs ensure children receive additional academic support.

Training Initiatives Details

Our vocational training programs include 3-month courses in tailoring, 6-month computer skills certification, agricultural best practices workshops, and traditional handicraft training. Each program includes practical hands-on training, business development guidance, and job placement assistance. We partner with local businesses to ensure our training meets market demands.
